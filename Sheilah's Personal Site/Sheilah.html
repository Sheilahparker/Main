House of Psalms: Becoming Her...

Today, I come with an open heart, and with gratitude for the knowledge and understanding to share and inspire. For it is my life purpose to be transparent in sharing my life's trials and tribulations. Sharing is truely one of the most important ways to encourage, uplift, and inspire others to endure and overcome. Allowing others into my life, required me to share and be transparent about personal struggles, but through an inward divine presence to guide me through the processes.

Over the past 11 years, I was inspired to use social media to display my transformational processes through daily scriptures. I choose to journal my inner inspired thoughts and sometimes displayed them with pictures to show the outer appearances. At the time, I did not know completely understand the complete ramifications of why, but I knew it was inspired from a presence bigger than I. .

Today, my hope and desire are to help others like myself to use divine presence to conquer their challenges which in many instances are fears and overcome them: grow through the process and then help someone else to do the same one day.
